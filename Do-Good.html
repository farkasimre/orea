Giving back

Nurturing prosperity, inspiring change

At Orea Capital, we believe that every financial decision has the power to spark positive change. Embracing a profound commitment to both fiscal wisdom and societal responsibility, we envision a future where our actions inspire positive ripples of change.

Our core pillars of responsible stewardship:

Our commitment to responsible stewardship revolves around three essential principles, inspired by the UN's guidelines on responsible investing. These pillars form the basis of our approach, as we strive to build an economic model that meets the needs of both present and future generations.

1. Environmental footprint

As an ESR (Entreprise Socialement Responsable), we don't just talk about eco-friendliness â€“ we live it, weaving sustainability into every aspect of our workplace, and beyond:

  • Our commitment is tangible in revamped waste management, a shift to digital-first for documents, and adopting eco-friendly packaging practices.
  • Our workspaces are powered by green electricity, marked by a conscientious approach to energy use. - Venture outside, and discover our green oasis fostering biodiversity. Our team finds respite in nature, surrounded by our flourishing vegetable garden. Driving sustainability, our fleet is steering toward a 100% electric future.
  • Since aligning with UNPRI in 2020, regular reporting underscores our commitment, turning sustainability into a compelling human promise.

2. Social responsibility

We prioritize people and purpose, passionately investing in the well- being of our employees, community, and society at large.

Our deep commitment to ethical investments guides our financial strategies, aligning with stringent ESG standards and emphasizing companies that create positive impacts on the environment and society.

Engaging in philanthropic initiatives and community involvement is integral to our values. We actively support local causes and initiatives fostering social empowerment.

In the workplace, we champion a harmonious work-life balance, promoting sports during the day and organizing family retreats and team- building events that highlight collective achievements.

3. Corporate governance

Recognizing the pivotal role of companies in achieving sustainable objectives, our corporate governance structures ensure that every action reflects our dedication to clients and societal well-being.

Our commitment extends beyond financial success, aiming to create shared value and foster a thriving business ecosystem.

Embracing continuous improvement, legal compliance, and ethical conduct, we contribute positively to economic sustainability.

Transparency is ingrained in our approach, nurturing trust for a harmonious working relationship.

Striving to be a beacon of responsible business, we uplift communities, leaving a lasting legacy of positive impact.

Helping hockey talents shine

Orea Capital proudly sponsors Hockey Club Luxembourg, a welcoming community founded in 1976. Our partnership focuses on supporting youths, ensuring they have the resources to pursue their dreams and stay on the right track. We're committed to empowering the next generation of leaders both on and off the field.

We proudly support:

Croix Rouge
Luxembourgoise

The Croix-Rouge Luxembourgeoise is a humanitarian organization committed to assisting vulnerable individuals in Luxembourg and worldwide. Through diverse projects and volunteers, they provide essential support, medical care, and disaster relief to those in the most dire need.

Missing Children
Europe

Missing Children Europe is a Federation for Missing and Sexually Exploited Children connecting 32 grassroot organisations in 27 countries across Europe. United, they are committed to the same goals: to prevent that children go missing, and to protect children from any violence, abuse or exploitation that leads to or results from them going missing.

Fondation
Roi Baudouin

The King Baudouin Foundation (KBF) is a Brussels-based foundation committed to fostering projects that have a meaningful impact on society. Established in 1976 to commemorate King Baudouin's reign, it supports projects locally, in Europe, and globally.

CPAG.ASA

PAG-ASA provides support to victims of human trafficking, offering a springboard towards autonomy and reintegration. They advocate for the rights of all victims, and fight for a Belgium where human trafficking no longer exists.

Charity
International

Charity International, Inc. is a Christian relief organization dedicated to working with children, families, and communities to overcome a lack of food, poverty and homelessness. Their reach extends to over 50 countries globally.

BIG (Breast
International Group)

PBIG Against Breast Cancer is a leading organization dedicated to advancing breast cancer research and treatment. With a focus on collaboration and innovation, BIG brings together researchers, clinicians, and advocates to accelerate progress in the fight against breast cancer.
