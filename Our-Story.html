ABOUT US

Our story

ABack in 2016, while the financial world hummed along to the same old tune, one banker dared to dream differently.

Tired of the antiquated banking system, and inspired by his grandfather who always told him to: "Prioritize your clients, and success will follow," our founder, Olivier Van Regemortel left his corporate banking job and founded Orea Capital.

Since then, Orea has blossomed from a small team of 3 in a single location to a robust team of 12 across 3 locations, serving clients in Belgium and Luxembourg with a unique blend of care, passion, and unwavering commitment.

“Orea Capital's exceptional performance and unwavering dedication to their clients deserve recognition. Alongside their outstanding financial results, they consistently demonstrate unparalleled support, accessibility, and attentive guidance over the years. Clients value the personalized care Orea Capital provides, fostering a deep sense of trust in their expertise. Orea Capital stands out as a beacon of excellence among private banks.”
