ABOUT US

Our values

At Orea Capital, our values are more than just words – they're the guiding principles that underpin everything we do. They reflect our commitment to offering our customers the very best level of service and support, at every step of the way.

Transparency

We see transparency as the cornerstone of trust and building lasting relationships. That's why we're dedicated to open communication, honest dealings, and providing our clients with clear and straightforward information at every turn. Whether we're discussing investment strategies, sharing financial insights, or addressing concerns, we ensure that our clients have the knowledge and confidence they need to make informed decisions.

Ethical integrity

Integrity is the bedrock of our business. We hold ourselves to the highest ethical standards, and always act in the best interest of our clients. This commitment extends to our investment practices, where we avoid involvement in industries such as arms, tobacco, and gambling. As finance professionals, we operate under the oversight of the CSSF (Commission de Surveillance du Secteur Financier), ensuring full compliance with relevant European laws and regulations.

Diversity, equity, and inclusion

We celebrate diversity in all its beautiful forms because we understand that every individual brings a unique and valuable perspective to the table. It's this diversity that enriches our team, our culture, and our ability to better serve our clients. Here at Orea Capital, we ensure that everyone – whether they work with us or for us – feels valued, welcome, and respected.

Social responsibility

We believe in making a positive impact on society and the environment. That's why we're committed to environmental stewardship, both within our office walls and beyond. Through sustainable practices, philanthropic initiatives, sponsorships, and responsible investing, we strive to create a better world for future generations.
